Frequently Asked Questions

Does this replace Composer?
No. Trade Conductor sits on top of Composer. Composer handles your strategy logic; we handle the risk management layer (stops, profit taking, circuit breakers) that Composer doesn't offer natively.
Is it safe to give API access?
Yes. While we require trading permissions to execute your risk rules, we explicitly do not ask for (and cannot use) "Withdrawal" permissions. Your funds can never be moved out of your account by our system. This is also a strong control provided by Composer.
What brokers are supported?
Currently, we support Composer whose algo trading platform is leading class for equities and ETFs. We plan to launch a copy-trading feature to trade your Composer strategies into Schwab for accounts that are not supported by Composer - like HSAs, 401ks, and Custodial accounts.
Can I backtest my risk rules?
Absolutely. Our "Time Machine" feature allows you to replay historical market data against your specific risk rules to see how they would have performed in 2020 or 2022.
Do I need a Composer account?
Yes. Trade Conductor requires an active Composer Trading Pass to function. If you don't have one yet, you can sign up for Composer here. Trade Conductor integrates directly with your Composer strategies to add the risk management layer.
